David Peña Vargas Flamenco Guitar Maestro €3499


  • Year: 2016
  • Model: Maestro no.23
  • Back and sides: Brazilian rosewood
  • Top: German spruce
  • Neck: Honduran cedar
  • Head and bridge: Indian rosewood
  • Fretboard: Ebony
  • Finish on top: Shellac
  • Finish on back and sides varnish (Nitrocellulose)
  • Hard case included

The wood selection for this guitar is German spruce for the soundboard which has aged for many years, and Brazilian back and sides which are extraordinary. The wood used in this instrument make it sound exquisite to your ears and provides power, depth and exceptional brilliance, offering an unmistakable flamenco sound.

David Peña Vargas was born in Jerez de la Frontera and at a young age is already considered one of the great promises of Spain. There are many artists who choose to play his guitars such as Manuel Valencia, Antonio Rey, Diego del Morao etc.
