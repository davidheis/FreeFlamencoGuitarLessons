Solea

The word solea is a gypsy abbreviation of soledad. The word soleares is an improper pluralization of “soledad”. Both “soleares” and “solea” signify loneliness and can be used interchangeably. The soleares has been described as the “mother of the cante”. There is no doubt that they are the central figure around which all of flamenco revolves.

There are countless theories concerning the antiquity and development of the soleares. It is thought that in some form, probably not that which we know today, the soleares have existed for many centuries.
