About Me

I started studying Front End Development as another career I could pursue as well as flamenco so this is a project I'm working on as I learn. I will share what I'm learning in flamenco with whoever is interested. I hope you get something out of my website!

I've been living in Jerez for a few years now. This does not mean you will become a flamenco master just from living here! But it does give you experience in how the flamenco world works. Maybe more on that later.

Anyway what I've learned I am now sharing with everyone. Education should be shared, not hidden away, only accesible to an exclusive few who can afford it. How many arts over history have been lost because people didn't want to share it with anyone outside their group?

I made this website so that whoever wants to learn, can, wherever they are in the world.
